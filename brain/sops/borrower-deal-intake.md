# SOP: Borrower Deal Intake

**File:** borrower-deal-intake.md
**Category:** Sales
**Status:** ✅ Active
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-05

---

## Purpose

Collect all necessary information from a borrower to evaluate a funding opportunity. Every deal must pass through this intake before any lender matching or structuring begins.

---

## Trigger

A borrower reaches out through any of the following channels:
- Social media / DM
- Website intake form (libertyequityx.com)
- Referral from Bryan Marriott, Brian Swinton, or other deal sources
- Investor communities (SubTo, Facebook groups, masterminds)

---

## Steps

### Step 1 — Receive Borrower Inquiry

Note the source channel and who referred them if applicable. Record in deal tracker (`brain/deals/deals.md`).

### Step 2 — Request Deal Information

Collect the following. If using a form, confirm all fields are complete before proceeding.

**Borrower Profile**
- [ ] Borrower name
- [ ] Credit score
- [ ] Liquidity (cash available for closing costs and reserves)
- [ ] Experience (number of prior deals)
- [ ] Entity name (LLC or Corp preferred)

**Property Details**
- [ ] Property address
- [ ] Property type (SFR, multifamily, commercial, etc.)
- [ ] Current condition

**Deal Financials**
- [ ] Purchase price
- [ ] Rehab budget
- [ ] Estimated ARV
- [ ] Loan amount requested
- [ ] Exit strategy (flip / refinance / rental hold / development)
- [ ] Timeline to close

### Step 3 — Record Deal in CRM

Add deal to `brain/deals/deals.md` with:
- Stage: 🔵 Intake / Screening
- Borrower contact file created or updated in `brain/people/`
- Source noted (who sent the deal)

### Step 4 — Confirm Receipt with Borrower

Send acknowledgment promptly. Keep it professional and direct.

**Example message:**
> "Thanks for sharing your deal details. I'll review the numbers and determine what funding options may be available. I'll follow up shortly."

### Step 5 — Pass to Deal Screening

Hand off to `deal-screening.md` SOP immediately after intake is complete.

---

## AI Instructions

- When a new deal is mentioned in conversation, run through this checklist and identify what information is missing
- Do not proceed to lender matching until intake checklist is complete
- If borrower provides partial info, flag the missing fields and request them before moving forward
- Record every new deal in `brain/deals/deals.md` under stage 🔵 Intake / Screening
