# SOP: Weekly Pipeline Review

**File:** weekly-pipeline-review.md
**Category:** Operations
**Status:** 🔴 Draft
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-05

---

## Purpose

Maintain full awareness of every active deal at all times. Catch stalled deals early, prioritize the right opportunities, and keep deal flow moving forward.

---

## Trigger

Once per week — recommended Monday morning or Friday end of day.

---

## Steps

### Step 1 — Pull Up the Full Pipeline

Open `brain/deals/deals.md` and review every active deal by stage.

| Stage | What to Check |
|-------|--------------|
| 🔵 Intake / Screening | Is intake complete? Has deal been scored? |
| 🟡 Structuring | Has capital stack been proposed to borrower? |
| 🟠 Submitted to Lender | Has lender responded? Is follow-up needed? |
| 🟢 Under Review | Any outstanding conditions or documents? |
| ✅ Funded | Fee collected? Relationship maintained? |

### Step 2 — Identify Stalled Deals

A deal is stalled if it has been in the same stage for **7+ days with no update**.

For each stalled deal:
- Identify the bottleneck (borrower, lender, missing documents)
- Send a follow-up immediately (see `borrower-follow-up.md`)
- Note the follow-up in the deal file

### Step 3 — Update Deal Notes

For every active deal, record:
- Date of last communication
- What was discussed or requested
- Clear next step with owner (Alex, borrower, or lender)

### Step 4 — Prioritize the Week

Rank active deals by proximity to funding.

Prioritize deals that are:
1. Under lender review with conditions outstanding
2. Awaiting borrower documents before submission
3. Recently scored A or B and ready for lender matching

### Step 5 — Check Deal Flow Health

Review overall pipeline volume:
- How many new deals came in this week?
- Is the pipeline above or below the 20–40 submissions/month target?
- If below target — trigger `deal-source-outreach.md`

### Step 6 — Update Goals File

If weekly progress is notable, update `brain/goals.md`:
- Move any quarterly objectives forward
- Update the "What's Slipping" section if needed

---

## Weekly Review Output

After completing the review, have a clear answer to:

1. What are the 3 deals closest to funding right now?
2. What is the single biggest bottleneck in the pipeline?
3. Does outreach need to increase this week?

---

## AI Instructions

- When asked for a pipeline update, run through this review process using `brain/deals/deals.md`
- Flag any deal that has been in the same stage for 7+ days as stalled
- Always end a pipeline review with the 3 priority deals and the top bottleneck
- Cross-reference deal source activity — if inbound is slow, flag it and suggest outreach
