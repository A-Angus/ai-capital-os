# SOP: Lender Relationship Management

**File:** lender-relationship-management.md
**Category:** Client Success
**Status:** 🔴 Draft
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-05

---

## Purpose

Maintain strong lender relationships so Liberty Equity Xchange always has reliable capital sources available when deals come in. Lender relationships determine execution speed — protect them.

---

## Trigger

- New lender relationship established
- Deal submitted to a lender
- Lender responds (approval, conditions, or decline)
- Monthly check-in cadence

---

## Steps

### Step 1 — Track All Lender Contacts

Every lender contact is logged in `brain/lenders/lenders.md` with a detailed profile file in `brain/people/` (using `_LENDER-TEMPLATE.md`).

Key info to keep current:
- Contact name and preferred communication method
- Current loan programs and criteria
- Active markets and any recent changes
- Deals currently in their pipeline

### Step 2 — Submit Only Qualified Deals

Never waste a lender's time with a D deal or a deal outside their criteria.

Before every submission, verify:
- [ ] Deal type matches lender's program
- [ ] Loan amount within their range
- [ ] Property is in their active market
- [ ] Borrower meets minimum credit and liquidity requirements
- [ ] Exit strategy is viable

Submitting bad deals damages the relationship. Quality over volume.

### Step 3 — Communicate Professionally on Every Submission

**Submission message example:**
> "Hey [Lender Name] — I have a [deal type] in [city, state] I'd like to run by you. Here's a quick summary:
>
> Address: [address]
> Purchase: $[X] | Rehab: $[X] | ARV: $[X]
> Loan request: $[X]
> Borrower credit: [score] | Liquidity: $[X]
> Exit: [flip / rental / refinance]
>
> Let me know if this fits your box and I'll send the full package."

### Step 4 — Follow Up on Submitted Deals

If no response within 48–72 hours:
> "Hey [Name] — just following up on the [property address] deal I sent over. Any initial thoughts on whether it fits?"

### Step 5 — Update Lender Criteria Database

When a lender shares new or updated program information:
- Update their profile file in `brain/people/`
- Update the relevant row in `brain/lenders/lenders.md`
- Note any markets they've added or removed

### Step 6 — Maintain Regular Contact

For active lenders, check in monthly even when no deal is in the pipeline.

**Check-in message:**
> "Hey [Name] — hope things are going well. Anything new on your end with programs or criteria? I have some deals in the pipeline and want to make sure I'm matching them correctly."

### Step 7 — Protect All Lender Relationships as Proprietary

Never share lender contact details with borrowers.
Never encourage borrowers to contact lenders directly.
All communication routes through Liberty Equity Xchange.

---

## AI Instructions

- When structuring a deal, always verify lender criteria before recommending them
- Flag if a lender hasn't been contacted in 30+ days
- Never suggest sharing lender contact info with a borrower under any circumstances
- When a lender declines a deal, note the reason in the deal file — it informs future matching
