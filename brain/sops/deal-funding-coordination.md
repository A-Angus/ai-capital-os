# SOP: Deal Funding Coordination

**File:** deal-funding-coordination.md
**Category:** Client Success
**Status:** 🔴 Draft
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-05

---

## Purpose

Coordinate the deal from lender approval through closing. Keep all parties aligned, conditions satisfied, and the deal on track to fund.

---

## Trigger

Lender has provided a term sheet, conditional approval, or letter of intent.

---

## Steps

### Step 1 — Review Lender Conditions

When lender approval comes in, immediately review all conditions required to close.

Common conditions:
- [ ] Appraisal or BPO
- [ ] Title report / title insurance
- [ ] Borrower documentation (ID, entity docs, financials)
- [ ] Insurance binder
- [ ] Signed purchase contract
- [ ] Rehab scope of work
- [ ] Comps to support ARV

### Step 2 — Communicate Conditions to Borrower

Send the borrower a clear list of what is needed and the timeline.

**Example:**
> "Good news — the lender has reviewed your deal and we're moving toward approval. Here's what they need to finalize:
>
> [List conditions]
>
> Please get these to me by [date] so we can keep the timeline on track."

### Step 3 — Track Condition Fulfillment

Update `brain/deals/deals.md` as each condition is satisfied.

| Condition | Status | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Appraisal | ⬜ Pending | |
| Title | ⬜ Pending | |
| Insurance | ⬜ Pending | |
| Borrower docs | ⬜ Pending | |

### Step 4 — Coordinate Between Parties

Act as the hub between borrower, lender, title, and any other parties.

- Borrower needs guidance → provide it
- Lender needs documents → collect from borrower and submit
- Title has questions → facilitate communication

### Step 5 — Confirm Closing Date

Once conditions are cleared, confirm the closing date with all parties.

### Step 6 — Track Fee Collection

Confirm Liberty Equity Xchange's placement fee or broker compensation is properly documented before closing.

- Fee amount: ____%  or $______
- Paid by: Borrower / Lender / Title at closing
- Confirm fee is on the closing disclosure (HUD / settlement statement)

### Step 7 — Post-Close Follow Up

After closing:
- Update deal stage to ✅ Funded in `brain/deals/deals.md`
- Record fee earned in revenue tracker
- Send a brief note to borrower and lender

**Borrower close message:**
> "Congrats on the close — glad we could make it happen. Looking forward to working on the next one."

**Lender close message:**
> "Deal closed — thanks for the smooth process on this one. I'll keep routing deals your way as they come in."

---

## AI Instructions

- When a deal reaches this stage, proactively list all outstanding conditions
- Flag any conditions that are overdue
- Always confirm the fee is documented before closing — never let it be an afterthought
- Update the funded deals table in `brain/deals/deals.md` immediately after close
