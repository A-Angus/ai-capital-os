## Lender Activation Process

For each target lender:

1. **Research** — confirm current programs, rates, and markets
2. **Introduce** — reach out via broker portal or direct contact
3. **Submit first deal** — establish the relationship with a clean submission
4. **Track response** — note close time, communication quality, reliability
5. **Add to database** — create profile in `brain/lenders/` using `_LENDER-TEMPLATE.md`
