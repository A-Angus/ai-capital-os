**Priority:** 🔴 High

## Purpose

Build a consistent pipeline of borrower deals that require capital placement. Without deal flow, even the best capital network cannot produce consistent income.

> **This project should receive the most attention right now.** The current bottleneck is limited deal volume due to W2 time constraints. Solving deal flow unlocks everything else — revenue, lender usage, and capital marketplace growth.

---

## Primary KPI

**20–40 deal submissions per month**

---

## Why This Project Matters

Revenue in a capital placement business is driven primarily by deal volume.

| Metric | Typical Range |
|--------|--------------|
| Loan size | $200k – $5M |
| Broker / placement fee | 1% – 3% |
| Revenue per deal | $2k – $150k |

---

## Deal Submission Flow

```
Borrower submits deal
        ↓
Deal intake form collects information
        ↓
Deal screening evaluates viability
        ↓
AI identifies potential capital structure
        ↓
Deal routed to lenders or capital partners
```
