## Project Summaries

### Borrower Intake System
Create a centralized system where borrowers submit deal information through a structured form that feeds directly into the CRM and capital placement workflow.

**Components:** Online intake form (libertyequityx.com) · Supabase backend · GHL CRM auto-population · Automated deal categorization and screening

**Why it matters:** Most deals fail because information is incomplete or scattered. This system becomes the front door of the capital marketplace — converting conversations into structured, actionable submissions.

---

### Capital Marketplace Platform
Create a system where borrowers submit deals through a portal and the platform evaluates deals and routes them to appropriate lenders.

**Components:** Borrower intake portal · Deal analyzer · Lender database · Automated deal routing · CRM pipeline tracking

**Long-term outcome:** Liberty Equity Xchange operates as a capital marketplace platform rather than a manual brokerage.

---

### Deal Flow Engine
Create a predictable pipeline of borrower deal submissions.

**Target:** 20–40 deal submissions per month

**Deal sources:** Investor communities · Wholesalers · Real estate agents · Operators · Referral partners

**Why it matters:** Deal flow is the primary driver of revenue and marketplace growth. Without it, nothing else functions.

---

### W2 Exit Plan
Replace W2 income through capital placement revenue.

**Milestone:** $15k monthly revenue for 3 consecutive months

**Strategies:** Increase deal flow · Close funding transactions · Build systems to automate deal processing

---

### Lender Network Expansion
Expand the number and diversity of funding sources.

**Focus:** DSCR lenders · Fix and flip lenders · Bridge lenders · Private capital partners · Family offices

**Outcome:** Ability to match a wider range of deals with appropriate capital sources. Goal of 3–5 reliable lenders per category.

---

### Capital Stack Intelligence System
Teach the AI how to structure capital stacks for real estate transactions.

**Includes:** Lender criteria database · Capital stack definitions · Deal analysis rules · Funding scenario modeling

**Outcome:** The AI can quickly identify viable funding structures for any deal type.

## How Projects Are Used

Projects track large, multi-step initiatives.

- **Individual deals** do not belong here — deals live in `brain/deals/deals.md`
- **Projects contain:** Strategy · Milestones · Progress notes · Next actions

---

## How to Add a Project

**Small projects** — add a row to the Active Projects table above.

**Complex projects** — create a dedicated file:
1. Copy `_TEMPLATE.md` → rename to `kebab-case-project-name.md`
2. Fill in scope, milestones, tasks, and next actions
3. Add a row to the Active Projects table above
4. Update `brain/README.md` directory map
