## Deal Source Strategy

### Goal

Build a network where multiple operators consistently send deals to Liberty Equity Xchange for funding solutions.

### Benefits
- Increases deal flow without proportional increase in outreach time
- Reduces need for direct prospecting
- Positions Liberty Equity Xchange as the go-to capital desk behind multiple operators

### Ideal Deal Source Profile

A strong deal source typically:
- Works with multiple investors
- Sees deals before financing is secured
- Needs funding solutions quickly
- Benefits from referral compensation or joint venture opportunities

---

## Referral / Partner Structure

When onboarding a deal source as a formal partner, Liberty Equity Xchange can offer:
- Referral compensation on funded deals
- Joint venture opportunities on select transactions
- Faster execution through established lender relationships
