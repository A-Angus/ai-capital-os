# Knowledge Base

> **Purpose:** Store your domain expertise here. When you discuss a topic with your AI, the relevant knowledge files are loaded on demand — making your AI an expert in YOUR business.

---

## How to Use

Create markdown files organized by topic. Your AI will reference these when relevant topics come up in conversation.

### Suggested Structure

```
knowledge-base/
├── README.md              ← You are here
├── your-industry/         ← Create folders by domain
│   ├── overview.md
│   ├── key-concepts.md
│   └── processes.md
├── your-product/          ← Your product/service knowledge
│   ├── features.md
│   ├── pricing.md
│   └── faq.md
└── your-market/           ← Market intelligence
    ├── competitors.md
    ├── trends.md
    └── opportunities.md
```

### Tips

- **Be specific** — The more detailed your knowledge files, the better your AI performs
- **Use headings** — AI scans headers to find relevant sections fast
- **One topic per file** — Easier to load on demand without bloating context
- **Update regularly** — Stale knowledge = stale AI responses
- **Brain dump it** — Just tell your AI "add this to the knowledge base" and it'll file it

| Industry | Knowledge Files You'd Create |
