# Calendar — Planning System

> **Purpose:** Map your year against goals, deadlines, and events. Bridge between what you want (goals.md) and what you're doing (daily/).

---

## How It Works

1. `goals.md` defines WHAT you want to achieve
2. `calendar/` maps WHEN it happens
3. Each month gets its own file: `YYYY-MM.md`
4. Your AI uses this to plan weeks and flag deadline pressure

---

## How Your AI Uses This

- **Morning:** Reads current week to report what's planned
- **Goal alignment:** Compares calendar against goals.md to flag gaps
- **Scheduling:** Slots new tasks into the right week
- **Deadline warnings:** Proactive alerts when something's approaching
