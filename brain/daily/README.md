# Daily Logs

> **Purpose:** Daily record of work done, decisions made, and lessons learned. Your AI can auto-generate these at end of day.
> **Format:** One file per day: `YYYY-MM-DD.md`

---

## How It Works

1. Throughout the day, your AI logs work as it happens
2. At end of day (or when you ask), AI creates a summary
3. Weekly reviews pull from these daily logs

---

## Daily Log Template

```markdown
# [YYYY-MM-DD] — [Day of Week]

## What Got Done
-

## Decisions Made
-

## Key Conversations
-

## Blockers / Issues
-

## Tomorrow's Top 3
1.
2.
3.

## Notes
-
```

---

## Commands

- "What did I do today?" → AI reads today's log
- "Log this: [thing]" → AI appends to today's log
- "Weekly review" → AI summarizes the past 7 daily logs

---

*Consistency beats perfection. Even a 3-line log is better than nothing.*
